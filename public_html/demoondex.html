Internet

Internet is a type of Wide Area Network spread over the entire globe. It is a very large network of thousands of smaller networks. Billions of computers relate to each other and communicate with each other and share resources. INTERNET is a short form of Interconnected Network of all the Web Servers Worldwide. It is also called the World Wide Web or simply the Web
